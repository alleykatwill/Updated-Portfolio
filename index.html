About Me

Thank you for taking time to stop and look at my portfolio. I am currently a student enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Chapel Hill. My portfolio contains links to some of the work that I have done as part of the program from the starting point through the middle of the program. While I have learned many new skills and languages through the program, I still have a lot more to learn. I look forward to my continued studies through the program and beyond.

I also have certifications from Johnston Community College in Spanish Language and Culture. I am currently employed at a pharmaceutical plant and have obtained my certification in BioWorks. My college courses have focused on business, Spanish and technology.
